Synopsis

Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is a prequel about high schooler Yuta Okkotsu, haunted by the powerful, vengeful spirit of his deceased childhood sweetheart, Rika Orimoto, who manifests as a monstrous curse. Recruited by Satoru Gojo to attend Jujutsu High, Yuta learns to control Rika's immense power alongside classmates Maki Zenin, Toge Inumaki, and Panda, all while facing the threat of the villainous Suguru Geto and his plan to unleash a curse-fueled apocalypse on the non-sorcerer world.
